Coleslaw Dressing

Coleslaw Dressing is a simple dish yet tasty.

It is very quick and easy fix to a tasty side-dish. Any age group can enjoy this dish and it is best in party's and get togethers

You can have immeadiately over a 14-ounce package of colosel mix or refrigerate until needed

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 and half tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 spoon black pepper powder
  • 1 table spoon salt

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  2. Tips:Use best measuring cups
